Proper pepperoni pizza ratios

Instructions

Step 1 - Gather Materials

  • Dough -Pillsbury
  • Pizza Roller
  • Sauce -Prego
  • Cheese- Mozzarela
  • Optional(recommended): Pepperoni -slice your own



Step 2 - Assemble Ingredients

Roll your dough to be about 1/2" thick with about 3/4" thick crust that is 1 inch wide. Place a thin layer of sauce on the pizza and spread evenly. Put copious amounts of cheese over the sauce, make sure you cover everything. Lastly spread your pepperoni evenly to your own liking.

Change it up with your toppings




Step 3 - Bake your pizza and enjoy!
