About




Why did I choose this project?

I have a deep passion for pizza and have my entire life. From a young age I have been blessed enough to enjoy possibly the greatest food invention of all time. I would recommend all people to at least try this delicousy at least once through their lifetime.




What advice do I have for beginners?

Pepperoni. That is the greatest advice I would give to any person who has never consumed pizza. Not only does it add protien to the dish, it also adds a flavorful burst that complements the cheese and crust alike. Along with this it makes this dish greasy which is my favorite attribute.
