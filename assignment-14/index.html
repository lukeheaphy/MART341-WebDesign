VNDS is a brand new store in Missoula, MT where you can find the best vintage and sneakers around. Luke Heaphy, our owner, has been curating a collection of streetwear pieces for the past 8 years. Now, he is wanting to share his passion with the world here at VNDS. Whether it be you're looking for rare jordans or the dunks that just dropped, VNDS is here to fufill your needs.











Our website will eventually contain our entire inventory so customers have an idea of what is in the store before they stop in. It will have a home page (this one), an about page with a contact form, a shoes page, a vintage page, and a hats page. All of the last 3 will have products that people can look at.
