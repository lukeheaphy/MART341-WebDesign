About VNDS

VNDS is a brand new store in Missoula, MT where you can find the best vintage and sneakers around. Luke Heaphy, our owner, has been curating a collection of streetwear pieces for the past 8 years. Now, he is wanting to share his passion with the world here at VNDS. Whether it be you're looking for rare jordans or the dunks that just dropped, VNDS is here to fulfill your needs.



We pride ourselves in our vast selection of vintage and rare sneakers. Our staff is knowledgeable and passionate about streetwear culture and can help you find the perfect pieces to fit your style. We also offer a wide selection of hats to choose from, so you can complete your look with the perfect accessory.
