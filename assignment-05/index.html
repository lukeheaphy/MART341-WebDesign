Hector and the Search for Happiness

Directed by Peter Chelsom

Hector and the Search for Happiness is the story of the psychiatrist named Hector who is on a lifelong quest to find what makes a person happy. Feeling as if he is a fraud of his profession in that none of his patients seem to get any happier. He sets of scouring the globe in search of the unanswerable question, what makes someone happy?

As Hector explores all possible explanations from lust to thrill, he comes to a warming conclusion. Love is the only feeling that triggers all of the emotions within the brain. Finding this answer somewhere familiar.
